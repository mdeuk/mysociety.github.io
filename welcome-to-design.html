Working with our

Design team

At mySociety, design is everyone’s responsibility

mySociety has a long history of transforming unfriendly digital systems into tools that are a pleasure to use. We’ve worked with governments, charities, big business and, of course, many partner projects around the world.

We’re accustomed to taking complex interfaces, user irritants and poor design choices, and replacing them with a simple, usable online experience.

We can do that because every member of our team brings a special combination of skills to the table: user research, data, their own expertise and industry best practices. You can talk to any member of our team about design matters, user research, or data gathering. Together, we can always find simple solutions for even the hardest of problems.

Design is the application of research, user data, expertise and best practice, to reach an optimal solution to a problem.

In that sense, every mySociety employee is a designer.

Our process

Don’t be concerned if we challenge your assumptions or suggestions.

When there is uncertainty about the way forward, user research is always the right answer

We’re not doing it to be awkward – we’re doing it because years of experience has taught us that successful products are always rooted in a clear understanding of what the user really requires.

When in doubt, we will always go back to your users. We’ll analyse real user data, conduct user research, or gather live user feedback to guide our decisions.

Guessing or making decisions on the user’s behalf can sometimes feel like an efficient shortcut, but if not done very carefully, it can introduce risks and doubt into the development process, and cause problems further down the line.
